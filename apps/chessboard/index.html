9x6 Chessboard Camera Calibration Pattern

Camera Calibration Tips:

  • Display the pattern on a high-resolution screen (e.g., tablet) or print it and mount it flat on a rigid board.
  • Ensure the pattern fills a significant portion of the camera view.
  • Capture images with the pattern tilted and at various angles.
  • Include images where the pattern is near the edges and corners of the camera's field of view.
  • Cover the expected working area and depth range of the camera.
  • Use good, even lighting and avoid glare on the pattern.
